LEGO NINJAGO

Cole’s Dragon

This set is great for small children and includes a storybook-style manual. It could use better posability for older fans.

The Crystal King

This season finale set features a powerful build with awesome trans-purple crystal pieces and highly detailed minifigures.

The Golden Dragon

One of the largest Ninjago dragons, featuring unique head molds, gold coloring, and a clever wing mechanism.

Nya’s Mech

A solid build with good proportions. However, it would benefit from added knee articulation and shoulder coverage.

The Source Dragon

The largest Ninjago dragon ever released. Fully poseable with a massive wingspan and strength to carry several others.

Zane’s Ice Dragon

A detailed dragon with cool blue tones, transparent wings, and spines running down its back. Visually very striking.

Thunderfang Dragon

The largest villainous Ninjago dragon. Designed to stand upright like a person, it has excellent articulation.

Jay’s Mech Dragon

This dragon shines with trans-neon green pieces and a unique missile-firing mechanism built into the chest.

Kai’s Mech

A powerful mech featuring four arms, great balance, and the ability to stand on one foot. Among LEGO's best mech designs.
