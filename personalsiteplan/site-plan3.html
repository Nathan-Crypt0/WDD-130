LEGO STAR WARS

AT-ST Walker

A fun build with great minifigures. Standing it up was tricky as a kid, but with age and care, it's a stable and impressive model.

B1 Battle Pack

The first true battle droid set, featuring great builds like the Octuptarra droid and outstanding 501st minifigures.

Clone Battle Pack

Includes excellent 501st clone minifigures and creative builds with alternate configurations for play or display.

AT-OT Dropship

A legendary and expensive set, featuring a detailed AT-OT walker and dropship combo. A rare gem for serious collectors.

UCS Razor Crest

An enormous, beautifully detailed model over 20 inches long. Features a carbonite rack, interior rooms, and a “baby on board” sticker for "Baby Yoda".

Imperial TIE Fighter

A sleek design, though some proportions are slightly off. Still a solid and iconic build from the Empire’s arsenal.

Millennium Falcon

One of the best sets LEGO has ever made. Fully furnished interior, great minifigures, and a must-have for Star Wars fans.
